Our Products

Biriyani

Savor the rich and aromatic flavors of our authentic Biryani, crafted with the finest ingredients and traditional spices. Each grain of basmati rice is infused with fragrant saffron, layered with tender, marinated meat or fresh vegetables, and slow-cooked to perfection. Served with raita and a side of tangy salan, our Biryani promises an irresistible experience that will leave you craving more. Whether you choose chicken, mutton, prawn, or veg, every bite is a journey of flavors and tradition.

Parotta

Experience the delight of our soft and layered Parotta, a South Indian favorite known for its crispy outside and fluffy inside. Made from finely kneaded dough, expertly folded and cooked to golden perfection, our Parotta pairs perfectly with spicy curries, rich gravies, or a simple side of chutney. Served hot and fresh, it's a treat you won't be able to resist! Try it with our signature Chicken Salna, Mutton Curry, or Veg Kurma for the ultimate indulgence.

Pizza

Indulge in our freshly baked, cheesy, and flavorful Pizza, made with a crispy, golden crust, rich tomato sauce, and a generous topping of premium ingredients. Whether you love the classic Margherita, spicy Peri-Peri Chicken, cheesy Veggie Delight, or meaty BBQ Feast, every bite is a burst of flavors! Topped with melted mozzarella and finished with aromatic herbs, our pizza is perfect for sharing (or keeping all to yourself!). Pair it with garlic bread and a refreshing drink for the ultimate treat.
